# Content Guide

## Tone of Voice
- **Welcoming:** "Welcome Back!", "Join the community"
- **Inspiring:** "Explore the World Like a Local"
- **Professional:** Clear error messages, no technical jargon in UI.

## Placeholders
- **Names:** "John Doe", "Jane Smith"
- **Emails:** "john@example.com"
- **Locations:** "Paris, France", "Tokyo, Japan"

## Button Text
- Action-oriented verbs: "Get Started", "Log In", "Register", "Continue"
